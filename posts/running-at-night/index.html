Nightcrawler

For the first time ever, I ran a 4k in the night at 11:30 PM around my apartment, that too after staying up for a software release. People talk about starting your day fresh after a run inconveniantly missing out the part about waking up early in the morning, and hustling after the run to get to work on time. In contrast when you run at night, you really don't have anything to get back to except your bed and when you do get back you obviously will sleep like a log cause you are tired after running. There are still some parts I don't like about running at night like being contrained to the apartment hence doing the same loop multiple times or not being able to look at where you step. I am definitely going on night runs more often.
